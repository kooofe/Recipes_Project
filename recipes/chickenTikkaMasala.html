Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a creamy tomato-based curry dish with marinated chicken pieces. It's a popular dish in Indian cuisine and around the world, known for its rich flavor and comforting taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder (optional, adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • (1) 14.5-ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional, for a richer sauce)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, ginger, turmeric, and chili powder (if using). Add chicken and stir to coat. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
  2. Cook the chicken: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  3. Saut√© the vegetables: Remove chicken from the pan and set aside. Add onion and garlic to the pan and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
  4. Add the tomatoes, coconut milk, and heavy cream (if using). Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Return the chicken to the pan and simmer for another 5 minutes, or until heated through.
  7. Serve garnished with chopped cilantro leaves and rice or naan bread.
